Welcome!

This Tethys App allows the user to select a possible mining location in southeastern Idaho and a proposed elevation for a mining cut. The Mining Explorer App then computes the volume of material to be removed and creates an updated DEM showing the cut.

The navigation buttons on the left-hand side of the page will guide you to the map, a help file, and technical and design documentation.

Click on Map on the left hand side to access the tool!
